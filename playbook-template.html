Executive Summary

This comprehensive playbook transforms the traditional paratransit software sales process into a strategic intelligence-gathering operation. By leveraging the Sales Enablement Platform's real-time market data, news insights, and opportunity tracking, sales teams can arrive at every meeting with deep contextual knowledge and tailored positioning.

The playbook integrates directly with platform capabilities:

  • Industry News (/news?filter=paratransit) for pre-meeting research
  • Sales Intelligence (/intelligence) for competitive positioning
  • RFP Database (/rfp-database) for procurement insights
  • Opportunities Hub (/opportunities) for pipeline tracking

Platform Integration Points

Pre-Meeting Intelligence Gathering

Before any customer engagement, leverage the platform's integrated intelligence:

News Analysis (/news)

  • Search for recent articles about the target agency
  • Filter by paratransit vertical for industry trends
  • Identify pain points mentioned in public statements

Agency Profile (/admin/agencies/:id)

  • Review fleet size, NTD data, and operational metrics
  • Track historical procurement patterns
  • Map relationships with providers and vendors

Opportunity Tracking (/opportunities)

  • Check for existing opportunities linked to the agency
  • Review related articles and intelligence reports
  • Assess competitive landscape from similar opportunities

Phase 0 — Data-Driven Preparation

Duration: 1-2 days
Platform Tools: News Database, Agency Profiles, RFP Archive

Enhanced Actions

  • Query /news?filter=paratransit for agency-specific articles from the past 90 days
  • Build capability map using insights from /product-portfolio/operations
  • Review similar-sized agency implementations in /reports
  • Extract competitive intelligence from recent RFPs in /rfp-database

Platform-Generated Artifacts

  • Auto-generated agency briefing from news aggregation
  • Competitive positioning matrix from market reports
  • Historical procurement timeline from RFP database
  • Peer agency comparison from NTD integration

Phase 1 — Intelligence-Informed Discovery

Duration: 60 minutes
Platform Support: Real-time intelligence feeds, agency dashboards

Platform-Enhanced Questions

Reference recent news when asking:

  • "We noticed your recent [article from /news] mentioned challenges with [specific issue]. How has that evolved?"
  • "Based on NTD data, your fleet utilization appears to be [metric]. Is that accurate?"

Real-Time Documentation

  • Log responses directly into /admin/opportunities/new
  • Tag relevant agencies and providers for relationship mapping
  • Create follow-up tasks linked to specific pain points

Phase 2 — Operational Intelligence Deep Dive

Duration: 45-90 minutes
Platform Tools: Operations Software Portfolio, Integration Guides

Data-Driven Discovery

Leverage platform insights:

  • "Other agencies your size typically process [X trips/day]. How does that compare?"
  • "We've seen success with [specific feature] at [reference from case studies]. Would that address your challenge?"

Workflow Mapping

  • Document current state in opportunity notes
  • Link to relevant product capabilities from /product-portfolio
  • Identify integration points with existing systems

Phase 3 — Technical Requirements Validation

Duration: 60-90 minutes
Platform Resources: Product documentation, compliance checklists

Feature Mapping Exercise

  • Use /product-portfolio/operations to demonstrate specific capabilities
  • Reference implementation success stories from /reports
  • Show real-world ROI data from similar deployments

Compliance Intelligence

  • Pull latest regulatory updates from /news?filter=government
  • Reference ADA compliance features from product portfolio
  • Link to relevant whitepapers in /whitepapers

Phase 4 — Procurement Intelligence Strategy

Duration: 30-60 minutes
Platform Tools: RFP Database, Government Procurement Guide

Strategic Questions Enhanced by Data

Based on /rfp-database patterns:

  • "We've noticed agencies in [state] typically use [procurement vehicle]. Does that apply here?"
  • "The average procurement cycle for similar systems is [X months]. What's your target?"

Procurement Mapping

  • Document in opportunity record with procurement type tag
  • Link to relevant procurement guides from /opportunities/government
  • Set automated alerts for RFP release

Phase 5 — Stakeholder Intelligence Mapping

Duration: 30-60 minutes per stakeholder
Platform Support: Stakeholder tracking, influence mapping

Multi-Level Engagement

Track in platform:

  • Create stakeholder profiles linked to agency
  • Document role-specific pain points
  • Map influence levels and decision authority
  • Set follow-up reminders by stakeholder

Phase 6 — Data-Driven Demo Development

Duration: 1-3 sessions
Platform Assets: Demo templates, ROI calculators

Demonstration Strategy

  • Pull agency-specific scenarios from news articles

Success Metrics

Phase 7 — Competitive Intelligence Activation

Duration: Ongoing
Platform Tools: Competitive analysis, win/loss database

Continuous Monitoring

  • Track competitive wins/losses by agency type

Phase 8 — Intelligent Proposal Development

Duration: 1-2 weeks
Platform Resources: Proposal templates, pricing tools

Dynamic Proposal Creation

Pricing Intelligence

Phase 9 — Reference Intelligence & Validation

Duration: 1-3 weeks

Strategic Reference Selection

Phase 10 — Implementation Intelligence Transfer

Duration: Post-signature

Seamless Handoff

  • Schedule success milestone reviews
  • Continue monitoring agency news
